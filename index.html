About Me

Welcome! I am Hitenkumar Kandoliya. My career has been a long and rewarding journey of solving complex puzzles, spanning over two decades in project management and software development. I’ve had the privilege of guiding everything from financial automation at Citi Bank to healthcare platforms and warehouse management systems, always with an eye on bridging the gap between what a business needs and what technology can deliver. Whether I'm wearing the hat of a Scrum Master, a Project Manager, or a Product Owner, my focus remains the same: leading teams with empathy, clearing their path, and ensuring we build something we’re all proud of. Lately, I've been channeling that same curiosity into the world of AI and Machine Learning, exploring how we can use these powerful tools thoughtfully and effectively.
